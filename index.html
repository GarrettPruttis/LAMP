The Pruttis Ranch is a family owned operation running cows on the High-Line of North Central Montana. The Ranch runs about 300 head of comercial mother cows on approximately 5,000 acres of prairie above the small town of Zurich Montana.

Our Philosophy

At Pruttis Ranch we are proud members of an organization called Ranching For Profit. Being part of this organization allows for a competitive edge in the industry. Some of these edges are:

  • Health and Productivity
  • Profitability
  • Quality
  • Management
